Project Summary

Pet parents often have trouble finding the right veterinarian, especially when they have pets other than dogs. All Pets All Vets is an app to make it easier to find and connect with the right vet.

My Responsibilities

A team of myself and two other UX Design students worked together on

  • User Research
  • Ideation
  • Prototyping

What we did

In this research project, we conducted one-on-one interviews with people who have or have had all kinds of animals, analysed the competition, and created and ran usability tests on prototypes.
